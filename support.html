Common questions

Is Brewd free?

Yes. Bean logging, brew logging, your taste profile, and café check-ins are all free. Brewd Pro adds AI bag scanning, advanced taste analytics, unlimited check-ins, and data export for €5/month or €30/year.

What platforms is Brewd available on?

Brewd is launching on Android first. iOS is planned and coming soon.

How do I delete my account?

Email contact@insightquestsolutions.com with your account email address and we'll permanently delete your account and all data within 30 days. This is irreversible.

Is my data safe?

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. We don't sell your data to anyone. See our privacy policy for full details.

I found a bug. What should I do?

Email us at support@brewdcoffee.app with what happened, what you expected, and your device model if possible. Screenshots help a lot.

Can I suggest a feature?

Yes — we genuinely want to hear from you. Email us. Feature requests from people who actually use the app shape the roadmap.
